Chicken and Rice

This chicken and rice recipe will blow your mind!

Try this recipe out for some moist chicken and rice.

  • Chicken
  • Instant rice
  • Vegetable oil
  • Preferred seasonings
  • One cup of water
  1. Thaw out chicken.
  2. Rinse chicken off with cold water then pat dry.
  3. Season your chicken with your choice of seasonings.
  4. Add an appropriate amount of oil to the pan.
  5. Heat oil to medium temperature.
  6. Add chicken and let it cook until it's almost done, flipping often.
  7. Once chicken is almost done, add rice.
  8. After adding rice, add your cup of water.
  9. Put lid over top of pan.
  10. Turn temperature up and let the mixture come to a boil.
  11. Turn the heat off and let it sit for 5 minutes.
  12. Ready to serve!
