Scrambled Eggs

"This scrambled egg recipe is the best i've ever had ~ Creator"

Are you tired of your scrambled eggs turning out dry? Give this recipe a try!

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Milk
  • Butter
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Crack your eggs into a bowl.
  2. Add a pinch of salt and some pepper into the bowl containing eggs.
  3. Add a splash of milk into the bowl.
  4. Melt butter in a medium-low pan.
  5. Add egg mixture into the pan.
  6. Cook on one side untl it's study enough to flip over.
  7. Flip eggs and scramble them to the desired size.
  8. Cook on low for 2 minutes.
  9. Ready to serve!
