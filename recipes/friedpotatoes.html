Pan Fried Potatoes

The perfect side dish to go with any breakfast meal!

This quick and tasty recipe will change the way you eat breakfast.

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Vegetable oil
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Garlic powder and Onion powder

Steps

  1. Clean your potatoes in cold water.
  2. Cut any "eyes" off of the potatoes.
  3. Slice potatoes to desired size with vegetable slicer.
  4. Season the potatoes.
  5. Heat oil to a medium temperature.
  6. Add potatoes into the oil.
  7. Cook until potatoes are hot and soft in the middle
  8. Ready to serve!
